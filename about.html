Hey, I'm Kevin Reedy!

Greetings Internet! I'm an infrastructure developer from Chicago. I currently work for Chef as a Customer Architect, where I help other companies be successful with their DevOps Journey and Chef's products. Since graduating from DePaul, I've worked for Belly, Riverbed, OpenDNS, and others.

In my free time, I enjoy brewing beer, snowboarding, playing shuffleboard, and promising myself that I'll update my blog more often. You can also find me on Github and Twitter.
